What is Project Ottershaw?

Project Ottershaw aims to fill a gap in the world of robotics by providing an unconditionally stable, highly mobile, self-navigating platform that performs tasks bipedal and wheeled robots have traditionally struggled with such as:

  • Navigation through damaged buildings
    • ​Rubble obstacles
    • Uneven terrain
    • Unstable walking surfaces
  • ​Multi-floor navigation
    • ​Sloped outdoor terrain
    • Stairs & rubble indoors
